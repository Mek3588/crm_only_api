Without Payment of an additional Premium, it is hereby declared and agreed that the company undertakes to pay compensation for bodily injury as hereinafter defined sustained by: one Driver and Other Four Occupants (i.e family members of the insured).

In the event of an accident happening to any motor vehicle (excluding motor- cycle) described in the schedule to the policy caused by violent, accidental, external and visible means which, independently of any other cause, shall within three calendar months of the occurrence of the occurrence of such injury result in:

1. DEATH: Maximum Amount of Birr {{deathBirr}} per Seat/Vehicle

2. PERMANENT DISABLEMENT: Birr {{disablementBirr}} multiplied by a percentage of amount stated under the scale of benefit described below.

3. MEDICL EXPENSE  Birr {{medicalBirr}} per person/per accident

Provided always that:- 

a) Compensation shall be payable under only one of items 1 to 3 of above in respect of any person arising out of any one occurrence and  the Total Liability of the Company shall not in the aggregate exceeds the Sum of Birr 50,000.00 during any one period of Insurance.        

b) No weekly compensation shall become payable until the total amount shall have been ascertained and agreed.

c) Such insured person is not less than 16 years or over 65 years of age at the time of such injury.

d) No compensation shall be payable in respect of death or injury directly or indirectly, wholly or in part arising or resulting from or traceable to;

i. intentional self-injury, suicide or attempt suicide or previous physical defect or infirmity or insanity,

ii. any accidental happening whilst such person is under the influence of intoxicating liquor or drugs.  

iii. If the claim is repudiated by driving license, carrying capacity, purpose, any form of limitations, etc. 

e) Such compensation shall be payable only to the injured person or to his or her legal representative, to the insured with the approval of the injured person or his or her legal representative and whose receipt shall be a full discharge in respect of the injury to such person. 


If the number of persons (including the driver) in the said motor vehicle, at the time of the occurrence, exceeds the seating capacity of the vehicle described in the Schedule of the policy, the Company shall be liable only for a pro-rata proportion of the compensation that would otherwise have been payable.
